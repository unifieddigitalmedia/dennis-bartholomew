DIVERISTY SPECIALISTS

The role of a corporate leader is to grow their business. To do so they will need to ensure that their customers, employees and partners are open to their products and services.

In the web-based world it is reckless to ignore the effect of words, images and actions that isolate your products or services from clients, work-team, potential employees and partners.

Dennis Bartholomew is a leading consultant involved in business development and improvement through the effective use of diversity and inclusion.

Some see equality as ‘creating a fairer society, where everyone can participate and has the opportunity to fulfil their potential’. Dennis Bartholomew views the elimination of unconscious preconceptions and bias as a key factor in corporate success. He has used his skills to deliver business improvements to a wide range of enterprises and organisations including national charities, local government, foreign governments and universities.
